Film

When I was in secondary school, I worked as a movie projector operator in a small cinema in my home town Oudenbosch for several years. I love watching good movies, and for a while I loved buying them as well. As a result, I own around 200 films on VHS (I know...) and DVD (I know...), many of which I haven't watched yet and probably never will. When I moved back to the Netherlands in 2014, I became the proud owner of a Cineville pass, giving me unlimited access to over 30 movie theaters in the Netherlands.
